How To Play

The values at the top of the screen describe a colour using the RGB colour model - the amount of Red, Green and Blue respectively in the target colour. 0 is the lowest value and 255 the highest. For example, RGB(255,0,0) describes a perfect red, RGB(0,255,0) a perfect green, and RGB(255,0,255) is a mix of a lot of red and a lot of blue, so you would expect the colour to be purple.

In easy Mode, you have 5 seconds to guess the colour once you press 'Go!'. In hard mode, you only have 3.
